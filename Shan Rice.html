Shan Rice

Ingredients & way to prepare for Making "Shan Rice"

Ingredients 

                    2 cups of rice
                    1 tablespoon of vegetable oil
                    1 onion, chopped
                    2 cloves garlic, minced
                    1 teaspoon of turmeric powder
                    1 teaspoon of ginger paste
                    1 cup of chicken or pork, diced
                    1-2 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
                    1 tablespoon of soy sauce
                    Salt to taste
                    Fresh cilantro and lime for garnish

Simple Step

                Rinse the rice: Wash the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.

                Cook the aromatics: Heat oil in a pot and saut√© onions, garlic, and ginger until fragrant.
                
                Add the meat: Stir in the diced chicken or pork and cook until browned.
                
                Season: Add turmeric powder, soy sauce, and salt to the meat mixture.
                
                Combine with rice: Stir in the rinsed rice and cook for a few minutes.
                
                Add broth: Pour in the broth, bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until rice is cooked and liquid is absorbed.
                
                Garnish and serve: Fluff the rice with a fork, and serve with fresh cilantro and a squeeze of lime.
